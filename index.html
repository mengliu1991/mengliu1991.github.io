Meng Liu (刘萌)

Professor, School of Computer and Artificial Intelligence, Shandong Jianzhu University

I am currently a Professor with the School of Computer and Artificial Intelligence, Shandong Jianzhu University. I received the Ph.D. degree from Shandong University, China, advised by Prof. Liqiang Nie and Prof. Baoquan Chen.

My research focuses on multimedia computing and information retrieval. I obtained my M.S. degree in Computational Mathematics from Dalian University of Technology, China, in 2016. I also worked as a research intern at the National University of Singapore from 2017 to 2018, advised by Prof. Tat-Seng Chua.
